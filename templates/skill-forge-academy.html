Overview

Skill Forge Academy is an educational landing page crafted to promote online courses, training programs, and learning platforms. It focuses on clarity, trust, and engagement to encourage learners to enroll. The layout emphasizes course value, instructor credibility, and learning outcomes. With structured sections and smooth flow, it helps users understand the learning journey. The design supports both technical and non-technical education products. Ideal for academies, bootcamps, and e-learning startups.

The Challenge

Online learners often feel overwhelmed by unclear course structures and lack of trust.

Our Solution

Skill Forge Academy presents courses in a clear, engaging, and confidence-building format.

Key Features

  • Course highlights
  • Instructor profiles
  • Learning outcomes
  • Enrollment CTA
  • Mobile-friendly design

Technology Stack

  • HTML5 — Structured course and content sections
  • CSS3 — Readable typography and layout system
  • JavaScript (Vanilla JS) — Interactive sections and smooth scroll
  • Responsive Design — Optimized for learners on all devices
  • Accessibility-Friendly Markup — Clear UI and spacing
